INTRODUCTION

From Kashmir to Kanyakumari in our India, there are different types of food. As you will see, there are certain menu items such as Idli-Dosa, Bhel-Chaat, Tea-Coffee, Samosa-Dhokla which you can eat wherever you go in India you will get it. So we chose a menu that would appeal to everyone. Whether it's Summer or Monsoon, or it's Delhi or Chennai or Mumbai or Calcutta, every place like Eat, that menu is IDLI-SAMBAR. This menu is eaten at any age, season, festival. So we choose IDLI-SAMBAR.

I am Mr. SURAJ SWAMI, born in Raibag in Karnataka and completed my education in Mysore. Rather than pursuing a job, I was inclined towards business. Hence, I pursued my education in BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) in 2018. During my education, I decided to start a business related to IDLI-SAMBAR.

We offer Traditional SOUTH SANSKRITI's flavor along with healthy fresh vegetables in our sambar. This has been well received by our customers here in Ichalkaranji. We receive daily orders from every corner in Ichalkaranji.
